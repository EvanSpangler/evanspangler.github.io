Complete IT Management for Small Businesses

Running a small business means wearing many hats. IT shouldn’t be one of them. We provide comprehensive technology management that keeps your systems running, your data secure, and your team productive.

Managed IT Services

Full-service IT management tailored to small business needs and budgets.

Ongoing Support & Maintenance

Day-to-Day IT Support - When something breaks or doesn’t work right, you need answers fast. We provide responsive support that keeps your team working.

Proactive System Maintenance - Regular maintenance prevents problems before they impact your business. Patch management, system updates, backups, and monitoring keep everything running smoothly.

Strategic IT Planning - Technology should support your business goals. We help you plan investments, avoid unnecessary spending, and build systems that grow with you.

Complete System Management

We handle your entire IT infrastructure so you can focus on your business:

  • Server Administration - Linux and Windows servers, virtualization, Active Directory, file sharing
  • Network Management - Routers, switches, firewalls, Wi-Fi, VPNs, remote access
  • Cloud Services - Email (Microsoft 365, Google Workspace), file storage, cloud applications
  • Backup & Recovery - Automated backups, disaster recovery planning, business continuity
  • Security Management - Firewalls, antivirus, patch management, security updates
  • User Management - Account administration, password resets, access controls, onboarding/offboarding

Communication Systems

Phone Systems - Modern VoIP solutions that work reliably and save money compared to traditional phone systems. FreePBX, Asterisk, or hosted solutions.

Email & Collaboration - Microsoft 365, Google Workspace, or self-hosted solutions. Migration, configuration, training, and ongoing management.

Web Design & Development

Professional Websites - Modern, mobile-responsive websites that represent your business well. Built with reliable, maintainable technology that you can update yourself.

Static Site Development - Fast, secure, cost-effective websites using modern static site generators. Perfect for business sites, portfolios, and informational pages. Lower hosting costs, better security, excellent performance.

Content Management - WordPress or other CMS solutions when you need frequent content updates. Secure configuration, hosting, maintenance, and training.

Technical Implementation - We handle the entire technical stack: domain registration, DNS management, SSL certificates, hosting configuration, email setup, and ongoing maintenance.

Practical Approach - We build sites that work well and are easy to maintain. No over-engineered solutions, no vendor lock-in, no ongoing dependencies on expensive platforms.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you require long-term contracts? No. We work on month-to-month agreements for managed services. Stay because we deliver value, not because you’re locked in.

What size businesses do you work with? Typically 5-50 employees. Small enough to care about each client, large enough to need proper IT management.

Do you work on-site or remotely? Both. Most day-to-day work happens remotely for faster response and lower costs. On-site service available when needed for installations, physical infrastructure, or complex issues.

How quickly do you respond to support requests? Managed services clients get priority support with response goals based on issue severity. Critical issues receive immediate attention. Routine requests typically handled within a few hours.

What if we already have some IT infrastructure? We work with what you have. We assess your current systems, identify what’s working well and what needs attention, and develop a practical path forward.

Can you help with specific projects even if we don’t need ongoing support? Yes. We handle one-time projects like migrations, upgrades, security improvements, and specific implementations.
